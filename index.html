HYPERBLACK COMMUNITY CONTRACT

WHO ARE WE :

HYPERBLACK is a street wear clothing brand based in Tunisia, established by youth and destined for youth. In the past years we noticed that the majority of us (Tunisian teenagers) have the potential of doing big things and achieving so far goals but since we live in a country that supports creativity to the point that it discriminates it we must create our own path alone.

That's why we started this brand, to implement the mentality of hustling in a drowning society.

In HYPERBLACK, the main priority is not and will not be financial gain. We sell a mindset, a lifestyle, not just pieces of designed clothing.

If you are a customer of our brand you will know that every single piece of work in the process of making your product is made with pure love and hope. Hope that the product will change something inside your soul and push you to do better.

Now we have a goal of gathering the biggest community of young hustlers & entrepreneurs in Tunisia and creating an atmosphere where we all can work on ourselves to evolve. That's why you are reading this article right now.

We started crafting the community and we cannot make it without you.

FOR DESIGNERS :

  • JOB DESCRIPTION :
    • This is like no other job you encountered before. Let's just keep it simple : you will be crafting the designs for the next products of the brands with dozens of other designers in the community. Along with this contract you will find another file, a google sheet for gathering the members' information. After one week of the releasing the sheet we will start choosing the designers that will join the brand. After that a discord community chat will be created to gather the chosen ones (who will get a participation prize the moment they enter the server). Once you are in you will find yourself working with an expanded team. But here is the twist, every designer will be working on his own design with a deadline, when the deadline is near we will pick from the designs worked by the community and the owners of those designs will be payed a percentage of every piece sold from their work which will create a strong competition between the members.

  • REQUIREMENTS :

    • Your age must be between 17 and 25.

    • Minimum knowledge of any designing software.

    • Creativity is a must. That's the only thing we will be focusing on not the experience.
    • Animation design is a plus.
    • Ability to work in a team.
  • WHAT DO WE OFFER :
    • A supportive environment where you can constantly work on upgrading your skills.
    • The ability to connect with a lot of people with the same passion and in the same field of yours.
    • In the last month our brand created some headquarters outside of Tunisia (Canada, Germany, Turkey, Serbia, Egypt...) so you will get the chance to make an international networking which is good for your future plans of work.
    • HYPERBLACK is not just a clothing brand , it's a long term goal which we are dedicated to accomplish. So you could be one of the founders of something very impactful in this world.
    • A flexible work time; you could work wherever and whenever you want.
    • You decide how much you will get paid based on your level of work, because we are not choosing only one design. So the more you work they more you get paid and so on.
